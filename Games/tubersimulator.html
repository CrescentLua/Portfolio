Tuber Simulator

❮ ❯

Tuber Simulator is a YouTube simulator game I made on Roblox using Roblox Studio and Rbx.lua (modified Lua). Within Tuber Simulator, players are able to create their own characters, customize them, create their channel, and begin building up their brand through uploading videos. Players are also greeted with a variety of upgrades that can increase efficiency with video creation. Aside from the standard gameplay loop, players have the ability to socialize with each other at a club. When it comes to the lore behind the game, I got inspired to make it in 2017 after playing a really addicting game by the name of Tube Tycoon. I originally created it for fun, but then realized the marketing potential the game had. After a few months, a handful of popular Roblox YouTubers found my game and uploaded their playthroughs on YouTube. The game became popular overnight with thousands of concurrent players playing Tuber Simulator. With all the support from the community, I was able to qualify for a Developer Exchange on Roblox (a program that Roblox offers that lets developers swap Roblox currency for real money). Through this, I was able to buy my first PC and probably wouldn't be pursuing an advanced diploma doing what I love! :)
